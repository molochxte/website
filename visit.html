Four Ways to Visit

We are open to our members twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week. In order to maintain safety and security within our space, visitors must be chaperoned and generally visit one of four different ways: going to our monthly(ish) open house, attending an event, scheduling an tour, or visiting as a member's guest.

Classes & Events

We run regular events, mixers, classes, open houses and more! Many of our events happen every month and even have their own micro communities. They can be a great way to make a new friend with a shared interest or get help with a relevant project you're working on.

Open Houses

We host an open house for tours on a monthly(ish) basis. If you are interested in checking out our makerspace, this is an excellent time to do so!

Member Guests

Our members are given monthly guest passes and may purchase more below. Member guests are chaperoned and guaranteed by that member, are not allowed to use tools, and must sign our waiver before entering the premises. If you are a member and would like to purchase more guest passes, you may do so here.

Tours & Open Houses
