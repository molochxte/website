Learn Something New

We are a nonprofit makerspace in the north end of the greater Seattle area. We are an inclusive community of makers, creators, and artists who get together to learn, share, and grow. All people, crafts, and skill levels are welcome.

Fast Facts

We operate an inclusive, affirming makerspace and maker community.

We are a 501c3 nonprofit and run entirely by volunteers.

Our member get makerspace access 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

We can be found at 12317 15th Ave NE #103, Seattle, WA 98125.

Schedule a Tour

What Drives Us

Our mission is to provide a friendly, inclusive, and affordable creative space and the tools for makers, artists, scientists, tinkerers, and learners of all ages to collaborate, hack, invent, share, create, and make. We are dedicated to sharing knowledge, building relationships, and fostering entrepreneurship and personal development, especially in the disciplines of Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math (S.T.E.A.M.).

Join a Movement

In an age of unparalleled access to information and technology, it's no surprise that more and more people are interested in becoming producers, rather than simply consumers. Makerspaces like ours do more than just host tools for prospective makers; they act as hubs for a larger community where people of all ages, backgrounds, and skills can come together and share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and make lifelong friends.
