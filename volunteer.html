We Are the North End Maker Volunteers!

We are the volunteers who make this community space hum, with inspiration, education, creativity and great collaboration.

Whether you want to share your resources, support our operations, or take on a leadership role.. JOIN OUR VOLUNTEER CREW to help keep it all happening!

Become a Steward

Are you an enthusiast for specific tools/areas in the makerspace? Consider becoming a Steward! Stewards are responsible for:

  • Training other members
  • Administering certifications
  • Maintaining our tools and equipment

For Non-Members

At this time, we don't have volunteer roles for non-members. Consider joining our community events or becoming a member to participate in more ways!
