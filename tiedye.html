Washing Tie Dye

We hope you enjoy your tie dye garment(s)! To finish them, you can follow these instructions or watch the video below:

  • 1. Let the dye set overnight.
  •  
  • 2. Begin rinsing your garment with cold water and remove the rubber bands as the water becomes clear.
  •  
  • 3. Gradually increase your water temperature to warm and rinse your garment until the water runs fairly clear.
  •  
  • 4. (Optional) Soak your garment(s) in warm water with blue Dawn dish soap.
  •  
  • 5. Run garments through 1-2 washing cycles on hot.
